# RenewAI: Business Case & Workforce Transformation

The transition to RenewAI represents a fundamental shift from volume-based processing to value-based customer relationship management. By automating 85% of routine outreach, we redistribute human talent to handle the most complex, high-stakes, and emotionally nuanced interactions.

## The New Team of 20 - Human Excellence at Scale

The transition from a team of 120 to 20 is a story of specialization. The remaining 20 members are the most experienced and empathetic professionals, focused on high-value and emotionally complex outcomes.

## Strategic Objectives

1.  **Redistribution to Value**: Human effort is removed from "reminders" and focused on "resolutions" and "retention."
2.  **Emotional Intelligence (EQ)**: Humans handle cases (distress, hardship, bereavement) where judgment and trust are paramount.
3.  **AI Stewardship**: The creation of "AI Operations" roles ensures the AI remains compliant, empathetic, and effective over time.
4.  **Regulatory Compliance**: Specialists stay focused on IRDAI and ombudsman interactions, backed by the AI's 100% auditable decision logs.
